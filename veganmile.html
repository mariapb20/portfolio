Problem context

Vegan diets have gained popularity in the last years. They have plenty of health benefits besides being sustainable for our planet.  There are many websites and apps that provide information on how to transition into a vegan diet, but they tend to have too much information and can be overwhelming. Users don't have a simple way to learn about veganism through educational interactions.

User

Target audience

From the research process and the topic of the site, the following target audience was defined:

  • People that want or are transitioning into a vegan diet.
  • People that want to make changes in their diet to improve their health (they are not necessarily looking to transition completely into a vegan diet).
  • People that want to start a more sustainable lifestyle.

User challenges

  • Find a website or app that summarizes the steps to start the transition into a vegan diet.
  • Have a clear knowledge of vegan substitutes for common foods.

Wireframes

User flows

Veganmile guides users through a main flow where they are invited to interact with each section in order to learn more about how to have a smooth transition into a vegan diet. Below is the user flow when users click in start vegan journey:

  1. 1.- Click on start vegan journey.
  2. 2.- Tap the elements to discover if they are vegan or not.
  3. 3.- Check the tips to have a smooth transition.
  4. 4.- Answer quiz question to discover vegan substitutes for your favorite foods.

Testing

Testing results

The testing process was done with 6 users (3 used the Desktop version and 3 the mobile version and app).  Main insights:

  • The tips section was well undestood but users complained it lack interaction. Due to this reason the tips were hidden under the picture, they will be shown once the user taps on the image (this can be seen in the mockups section).
  • The content was well received and users thought it was really educational.
  • The interactions were well understood by the users.

Redesign

  • From the feedback received on the testing phase, the layout of the tips section was modified to allow more interaction with the users.
  • The results helped improve the design and the changes were applied to the mockups presented in the following section.

UI Design

The goal was to present a design that gave a calm and fresh look.

Color palette

A pastel color was used in some sections of the background to give rhythm to the site. For the CTA we use a bright red to invite the user to interact with the site. The color inspiration came from fruits such as strawberries and pomegranates.

Fonts

For the fonts, I used Foco and Foco light for the headers (h1 and h2) and Avenir Next for the h3 and text bodies. I chose Foco since is a playful font that has high legibility. Avenir next seemed like a good complement to Foco since its simple and is easy to read.

Outcomes & lessons learnt

  • Veganmile is an app and website that provides a place where people can easily learn about veganism through simple interactions.
  • Implementing the website design in 3 screens made it simple for the users to understand the contents and activities of the site while encouraging them to download the app.
  • The flow of the app and website were easy to follow and the users did not felt overwhelmeded with the information since it was categorized.
  • As a next step we could further improve the tips version so its more interactive for users.
