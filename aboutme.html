Hi there! My name is María and I'm a UX / UI designer based in Barcelona.

I'm from Venezuela and back there I got my degree in electronic engineering. In 2014, I moved to Barcelona to get my master's degree in telecommunication engineering.

Although I love the world of networks and telecommunications and over the last 6 years I have been gaining experience in operations and customer support, I have a creative side that I wanted to explore so I decided to start a new path.

Professionally, I have always been involved in the last stages of a product. Meaning when the product is launch and the users get to interact with it and we hear their feedback. This experience was really rewarding and it has allowed me to learn to listen to the customers, understand their needs and provide them timely solutions to problems they may be encountering with the product.

All of these lessons have really motivated me and inspired me to develop myself in product design. I aim to design products that focus on understanding users' struggles and motivations in order to offer them a useful and usable design that provides a joyful and engaging experience.
